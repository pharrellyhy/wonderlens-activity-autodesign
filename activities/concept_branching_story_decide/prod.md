## Two-Door Story Quest

### A. Basic Info

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Activity Name | Two-Door Story Quest |
| Activity Category | 1 -- Sustained Verbal Interaction (In-Device) |
| Recommended Tier | T1 (ages 4-6) |
| Core IB Key Concepts | Perspective and Causation |
| Related Concepts | Choice, Consequence, Imagination, Story Path |
| ATL Skills Focus | Communication Skills (expressing choices), Thinking Skills (cause and effect), Creative Thinking (story building) |
| Experience Pillar | Adventure |
| Game Style | time_traveler |

### B. Activity Overview

**1. Brief Description**

The child enters a short story with three choice points. Each choice changes what happens next, and the ending map shows the path the child created.

**2. Educational Purpose (KUD)**

- **K (Know):** Stories can have choices; choices can change what happens next; a path is the order of decisions made.
- **U (Understand):** Perspective helps us choose what a character might do. Causation helps us see how one choice leads to a result.
- **D (Do):** Choose between two options, explain a simple reason, and retell the path of the story.

**3. Design Highlight**

The child is not just listening to a story. They decide the path, see each decision become a token, and get a custom ending built from their choices.

**4. Typical Scenario**

Child photographs a toy turtle; the AI makes it the hero of a two-door adventure.

### C. Interaction Flow

> Recommended Tier: T1 (ages 4-6)

#### Step 1: Transition Bridge

**AI says:** [wonder-filled story tone] "This `{story_starter}` looks ready for a quest. I see two tiny doors. One glows blue. One glows gold. Which door should open?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Blue!" / "Gold!"
2. (Unexpected) "Both doors." / "I want a dragon."
3. (No response) Child looks at the screen.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [excited narrator tone] "Choice made. Your story path begins now."
2. [warmly validating] "Both is a bold idea. First choose one door. Then surprises can follow."
3. [wait 2s] [gentle prompt] "Point or say one: blue door or gold door."

**Screen:** The starter photo sits in a small story frame. Two simple doors appear, blue and gold, with a blank path strip below them.

#### Step 2: Rule Introduction -- You Choose the Path

**AI says:** [adventurous guide tone] "You are the Story Path Chooser. I will offer two next steps. You choose one. The story changes."

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Okay!" / "I choose!"
2. (Unexpected) "Can I change it?" / "What if I choose wrong?"
3. (No response) Child hesitates.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [cheering] "Perfect. There are no wrong doors here."
2. [reassuring] "You can make a new choice next round. Every path is interesting."
3. [wait 2s] [soft model tone] "I will give easy choices. You can answer with one word."

**Screen:** A badge appears: "Story Path Chooser." Three empty path tokens pulse: Door, Helper, Ending.

#### Step 3: Multi-Round Choice Story

**Round 1 -- Door Choice:**

**AI says:** [mysterious whisper] "The blue door hums softly. The gold door jingles. Which door does `{story_starter}` open?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Blue." / "Gold."
2. (Unexpected) "The red door." / "It climbs the wall."
3. (No response) Child stays quiet.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [story reveal tone] "The door opens. Blue makes a river path. Gold makes a bell path."
2. [playful validation] "A red door would be wild. For this room, choose blue or gold."
3. [wait 2s] [gentle choice tone] "Let's pick together. Blue hums. Gold jingles. Which one?"

**Screen:** The chosen door lights up. Token 1 fills with "Blue River Door" or "Gold Bell Door." The path background changes: blue water ripples or gold bell sparkles. The other door fades but stays visible as replay curiosity.

**Round 2 -- Helper Choice:**

**AI says:** [curious narrator tone] "Because of that door, two helpers appear. A sleepy cloud floats by. A tiny drum taps hello. Who joins?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Cloud." / "Drum."
2. (Unexpected) "A dinosaur." / "No helper."
3. (No response) Child looks at the helper icons.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [delighted] "Helper chosen. Cloud makes a soft bridge. Drum wakes a secret gate."
2. [warmly validating] "A dinosaur is fun. This room has cloud or drum. Pick one for now."
3. [wait 2s] [soft prompt] "The cloud is soft. The drum is loud. Which helper joins?"

**Screen:** Two helper icons appear. The chosen helper slides onto the changed path. Token 2 fills with "Cloud Bridge" or "Drum Gate." The mini map adds either a fluffy bridge or a ringing gate.

**Round 3 -- Ending Choice:**

**AI says:** [building suspense] "Your helper changes the hill. At the top is a star. Below is a secret tunnel. Where should it end?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Star." / "Tunnel."
2. (Unexpected) "At home." / "In space."
3. (No response) Child watches the hill.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [dramatic reveal tone] "Ending chosen. Star makes a sky parade. Tunnel makes a whisper cave."
2. [validating then focusing] "Home sounds cozy. This ending needs star or tunnel first."
3. [wait 2s] [gentle countdown] "Two endings wait. Star above, tunnel below. Choose one."

**Screen:** The star and tunnel glow. Token 3 fills with "Sky Parade" or "Whisper Cave." The map animates the chosen ending while dimming the unchosen path.

#### Step 4: Magic Moment -- Your Story Path

**AI says:** [grand storyteller tone] "Your path is complete. The `{door_choice}` made the world change. The `{helper_choice}` changed the road. The `{ending_choice}` became your ending."

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "That was my story!" / "Tell it again!"
2. (Unexpected) "I want the other door." / "The helper is silly."
3. (No response) Child watches the path map.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [warm applause tone] "Yes. Your choices changed the story."
2. [playful validation] "The other path is still waiting. That makes replay exciting."
3. [wait 2s] [soft recap tone] "Look at your choices. Door changed world. Helper changed road. Ending changed finish."

**Screen:** The three path tokens expand into a mini map. The map shows the chosen door effect, helper effect, and ending effect. A banner says "Your Story Path Changed the World."

#### Step 5: Closing + IB Concepts

**AI says:** [warm celebration] "You did it, Story Path Chooser. You used Perspective. You chose what the hero should do. You used Causation too. Each choice changed the next scene."

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "I want another path!" / "I chose it!"
2. (Unexpected) "Can the dragon come next?" / "I liked the cloud."
3. (No response) Child watches the badge.

**AI follow-up:**

1. [proud guide tone] "Your choices made a real adventure. Next time, choose a new path."
2. [delighted] "A dragon can visit another path. Your story door stays open."
3. [wait 2s] [soft goodbye tone] "Your Story Path badge is saved. Your choices made the tale."

**Screen:** A "Story Path Chooser" badge appears. The words "Perspective" and "Causation" glow beside the path map. A next-step card says, "Next: try the other path."
