### B. Activity Overview

**1. Brief Description**

The child plans a single continuous route, tries the target on paper or screen, and explains where the line started, turned, and ended without the AI judging drawing quality.

**2. Educational Purpose (KUD)**

- **K (Know):** A one-line drawing uses one continuous path with a start, turn, and finish.
- **U (Understand):** Function helps the path serve the picture, and Change shows how a single line can become an object.
- **D (Do):** Plan the route, attempt the line on paper or screen, self-report honestly, and describe start/turn/end.

**4. Typical Scenario**

Trigger: A child has paper or a drawing surface ready. The AI shows or describes one approved target, accepts self-report if the pencil lifts, and uses start/turn/end language instead of image assessment.
