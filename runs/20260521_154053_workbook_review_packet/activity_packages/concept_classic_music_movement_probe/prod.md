### B. Activity Overview

**1. Brief Description**

Child moves their body in response to music mood or rhythm cues. The child acts as the Mood Mover and completes 2 safe movement-and-voice turns before seeing two safe mood movements and one calm freeze badge.

**2. Educational Purpose (KUD)**

- **K (Know):** Safe movement needs space, a stop option, and small body choices.
- **U (Understand):** Form helps the child notice rhythm or mood shape, and Perspective lets the child show that mood safely.
- **D (Do):** Try two low-risk movements, self-report the mood, freeze safely, and reflect on the movement path.

**3. Design Highlight**

This package is generated under `minimum_unblock_allowed`, so former blockers are visible but resolved. No displayed asset is required; progress is represented with simple in-session prompt and recap state.

**4. Typical Scenario**

Trigger: A parent starts a short movement break in an open, calm space. The AI offers rhythm or mood cues without promising copyrighted playback or motion detection, and the child chooses gentle sway and freeze options.
