## Activity: Kitty's Secret Sounds

### A. Basic Info

- **Activity Name**: Kitty's Secret Sounds
- **Activity Category**: 1 — Sustained Verbal Interaction (In-Device)
- **Recommended Tier**: T0 (ages 2-4)
- **Core IB Key Concepts**: **Perspective** (What are the points of view?) & **Connection** (How is it connected to other things?)
- **Related Concepts (Discipline)**: Emotion, Expression, Imagination, Empathy
- **ATL Skills Focus**: Communication Skills (expressing ideas, listening), Thinking Skills (creative thinking), Social Skills (empathy — understanding another's feelings)
- **Trigger Entity**: Stuffed cat
- **Trigger Scene**: Child photographs their stuffed cat on the bed

### B. Activity Overview

- **① Brief Description**: After the child photographs their stuffed cat, the AI whispers that cats have secret sounds for different feelings. The child becomes the cat's "voice" — making special sounds for how kitty feels in cozy little scenarios. Each round, the AI paints a tiny scene and the child says what kitty sounds like.

- **② Educational Purpose (KUD)**:
  - **K (Know)**: Learn that cats purr, meow, and hiss. Learn that different sounds can mean different feelings (happy, curious, sleepy). Learn the words "cozy," "purr," and "snuggle."
  - **U (Understand)**: Understand that the same cat can feel different ways in different moments (**Perspective**). Understand that sounds and feelings are **Connected** — a sound tells you how someone feels.
  - **D (Do)**: Practice creative vocal expression by making animal sounds (Communication Skills — expressing). Practice imagining how another being feels (Social Skills — empathy). Practice associating scenarios with emotions (Thinking Skills — creative thinking).

- **③ Design Highlight**: The "secret sounds" metaphor turns emotional vocabulary practice into a sensory, sound-making game perfect for toddlers. Children already love making animal sounds — this activity channels that into emotional expression. Each round uses a different simple scenario (warm blanket, loud noise, belly rub) that toddlers viscerally understand.

- **④ Typical Scenario**: Child photographs their stuffed cat on the bed → AI notices how snuggly kitty looks → launches a call-and-response game where the child makes kitty's secret sounds for different feelings.

### C. Interaction Flow — Detailed Design [Target Tier: T0 (ages 2-4)]

**Step 1: Transition Bridge**

> **AI says**: "*(soft, warm gasp)* Ohhh! Kitty! So snuggly!"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "That's my kitty!" / "Yeah!" / Names the cat.
> 2. (Unexpected) "Meow!" / "It's soft!"
> 3. (No response) Looks at the screen quietly.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(cozy whisper)* Your kitty looks sooo comfy! Is kitty sleepy right now?"
> 2. "*(delighted)* Meow! I love that! Kitty looks so cozy there. Is kitty happy?"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(gentle whisper)* Your kitty looks warm. Soooo cozy on the bed!"
>
> **Screen**: The stuffed cat photo fills the screen with a soft, warm glow around the edges. Tiny animated "zzz" bubbles float gently upward. A faint lullaby-style twinkle plays.

**Step 2: Rule Introduction + Demo**

> **AI says**: "*(excited whisper)* Guess what? Kitty has secret sounds! Happy kitty goes... PURRRR! Can you say PURRRR?"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "Purrrr!" / Makes a purring sound.
> 2. (Unexpected) "Meow!" / "Kitty!" / Random sound.
> 3. (No response) Stays quiet, listens.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(thrilled)* PURRRR! Yes! You sound just like kitty! Okay — I tell a story, you make kitty's sound. Ready?"
> 2. "*(giggling)* Ooh, that's a good sound too! Kitty has LOTS of sounds. I'll tell kitty stories — you make the sound! Ready?"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(playful)* PURRRR! That's happy kitty. Let's play! I tell a story. You be kitty's voice!"
>
> **Screen**: The stuffed cat photo is centered. A small animated speech bubble appears near the cat showing "PURRRR" in wiggly letters. The bubble bounces playfully, then fades.

**Step 3: Multi-Round Interaction (2 rounds)**

> **Round 1 — Cozy Kitty (happy/relaxed):**
>
> **AI says**: "*(warm, storytelling tone)* Kitty is in a warm blanket. Sooo snuggly! What sound does kitty make?"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "Purrrr!" / "Mmmm!" / A cozy sound.
> 2. (Unexpected) "Meow!" / "Sleeping!" / Any word.
> 3. (No response) Silent.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(melting)* PURRRR! So warm and happy! Kitty loves that blanket!"
> 2. "*(validating)* Yes! Kitty says meow! Like — 'I love this blanket!' So cozy!"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(soft)* Maybe kitty goes... purrrr? Nice and warm! Purrrr!"
>
> **Screen**: A cozy blanket illustration wraps softly around the cat photo. Warm orange and pink tones. Small heart shapes drift upward.

> **Round 2 — Curious Kitty (curious/alert):**
>
> **AI says**: "*(mysterious whisper)* Oh! Kitty hears a little sound. What's that? What does kitty say?"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "Mrrrow?" / "Meow?" / A questioning sound.
> 2. (Unexpected) "A bird!" / "Purr!" / Random answer.
> 3. (No response) Silent.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(delighted)* Mrrrow? Kitty is SO curious! 'What was that noise?' Good ears, kitty!"
> 2. "*(building on it)* Ooh, maybe it IS a bird! Kitty hears it and goes 'mrrrow?' So curious!"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(playful)* Maybe kitty goes 'mrrrow?' Like — 'what's that?' So curious!"
>
> **Screen**: The cat photo has two animated ears that perk up. A small question mark floats above. The background shifts to a soft blue-green "alert" tone.

> **Round 3 (optional, if child is engaged) — Hungry Kitty (wanting/excited):**
>
> **AI says**: "*(playful gasp)* It's dinner time! Kitty smells yummy food! What does kitty say?"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "MEOW! MEOW!" / "Maaaow!" / An eager sound.
> 2. (Unexpected) "Fish!" / "Yummy!" / "I'm hungry too!"
> 3. (No response) Silent.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(laughing)* MEOW MEOW! 'Give me food!' Hungry kitty is LOUD! Haha!"
> 2. "*(giggling)* Fish! Yummy! Kitty goes MEOW MEOW! 'I want some too!'"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(enthusiastic)* Hungry kitty goes MEOW MEOW! So loud! 'Feed me!'"
>
> **Screen**: A tiny animated fish or food bowl appears near the cat. The cat photo gently bounces. Bright, warm yellow background tones.

> **Round 4 (optional, if child is engaged) — Scared Kitty:**
>
> **AI says**: "*(dramatic whisper)* Oh no! A big loud BOOM! Thunder! What does kitty say?"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) "HISSS!" / "Meooww!" / A scared sound.
> 2. (Unexpected) "Hide!" / "Under the bed!" / "I'm scared too!"
> 3. (No response) Silent.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(sympathetic)* Ohhh! Poor kitty! HISSS means 'I'm scared!' It's okay, kitty. Hugs!"
> 2. "*(warm)* Yes! Kitty hides! And goes 'hisss!' That means 'too loud!' But you're here — kitty is safe!"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(gentle)* Scared kitty goes hisss! But don't worry. You keep kitty safe!"
>
> **Screen**: The background flashes briefly gray with a small lightning bolt, then softens. The cat photo has a small animated tail that poofs up. A heart appears as AI reassures.

**Step 4: Celebration + Role Title**

> **AI says**: "*(proud, celebrating)* WOW! You know ALL kitty's sounds! You are... Kitty's Voice!"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Ideal) Giggles, repeats "Kitty's Voice!", makes more sounds.
> 2. (Unexpected) "More!" / "Again!" / "Purrrr!"
> 3. (No response) Smiles or stays quiet.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(cheering)* Kitty's Voice! Purr, meow, hiss — you know them all!"
> 2. "*(warm)* You love being kitty's voice! You made so many great sounds!"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(gentle celebration)* You made kitty talk! Happy purrs, big meows — amazing!"
>
> **Screen**: A golden badge animation appears showing a cat silhouette with sound waves around it. Text reads "Kitty's Voice" in playful, rounded letters. Sparkles and gentle confetti fall. A cheerful chime plays.

**Step 5: Closing + IB Concepts**

> **AI says**: "*(warm, reflective)* You showed how kitty feels! Happy, curious, hungry! Same kitty, many feelings. That's **Perspective**! Every feeling has a sound. That's **Connection**! Yay, Kitty's Voice!"
>
> **Possible child responses**:
> 1. (Engaged) "Purrrr!" / "Bye kitty!" / Hugs the stuffed cat.
> 2. (Quiet) Smiles or says nothing.
> 3. (No response) Child cuddles the stuffed cat or watches quietly.
>
> **AI follow-up**:
> 1. "*(warm goodbye)* PURRRR! Kitty says thank you! Bye bye, Kitty's Voice!"
> 2. "*(soft)* Give kitty a big hug! Bye bye, Kitty's Voice!"
> 3. *(waits 2 seconds)* "*(soft)* Kitty looks extra cozy with you. Bye bye, Kitty's Voice!"
>
> **Screen**: The cat photo returns to center with all four scenario icons arranged around it (blanket, question mark, food bowl, lightning bolt) connected by gentle dotted lines. The words **"Perspective"** and **"Connection"** float upward in soft, artistic lettering — "Perspective" styled with little cat ears on the P, "Connection" with a dotted line linking the letters. A gentle lullaby note plays as the screen fades warmly.
