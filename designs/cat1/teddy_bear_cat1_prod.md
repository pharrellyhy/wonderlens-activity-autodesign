### B. Activity Overview

**① Brief Description**

After the child photographs their teddy bear on the bed, the AI notices how cozy it looks and wonders if teddy needs help getting ready for bed. The child becomes teddy's "bedtime helper" — telling teddy what to do in tiny bedtime scenes. Each round, the AI describes a bedtime moment and the child tells teddy what to do or how teddy feels, practicing caring and comfort language through call-and-response.

**② Educational Purpose (KUD)**

- **K (Know)**: Learn the words "cozy," "soft," "hug," "safe," and "gentle." Learn that teddy bears are soft and fluffy and made for hugging.
- **U (Understand)**: Understand that a teddy bear's job is to give comfort and feel cozy — that's its Function. Understand that taking care of teddy (being gentle, keeping it safe) is our Responsibility.
- **D (Do)**: Practice expressing care through simple verbal instructions. Practice empathetic caregiving language like "gentle" and "shh." Practice calming self-talk through bedtime role-play.

**③ Design Highlight**

The "bedtime helper" role transforms a simple stuffed animal into a character the child must care for. Instead of quizzing the child about the bear, the AI creates tiny bedtime moments — each one a chance for the child to practice nurturing language. The child doesn't just describe teddy; they HELP teddy, building a sense of responsibility through play. Every round uses sensory details (soft fur, warm hugs, gentle pats) that toddlers feel in their bodies.

**④ Typical Scenario**

Child photographs their teddy bear on the bed → AI notices teddy looks sleepy → child becomes teddy's bedtime helper, guiding teddy through cozy bedtime moments with sounds and caring words.

### C. Interaction Flow

> Recommended Tier: T0 (ages 2–4)

#### Step 1: Transition Bridge

**AI says:** (delighted gasp) "Oh! Teddy bear! Soft and cozy. Sleepy today?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Yes, teddy sleepy!" / "Teddy wants bed!"
2. (Unexpected) "Teddy is playing!" / "No, teddy is happy!"
3. (No response) Child is silent or looking at teddy.

**AI follow-up:**

1. (cozy whisper) "Aww! Sleepy teddy! Help teddy sleep?"
2. (giggly) "Happy teddy! Bedtime soon. Get cozy?"
3. (wait 2s) (soft, inviting) "Look at teddy! So fluffy. Can you say hi?"

**Screen:** Teddy bear photo centered with soft blue-purple night sky background, twinkling stars, and a small moon peeking from the corner.

#### Step 2: Rule Introduction + Demo

**AI says:** (excited whisper) "Here's the game! I say need. You help. Teddy cold. Brrr! Say, 'Hug teddy!'"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "Hug teddy!" / child makes hugging sounds / "Squeeeze!"
2. (Unexpected) "Teddy bear!" / just repeats "teddy"
3. (No response) Child is quiet.

**AI follow-up:**

1. (warm celebration) "Yes! Big hug! Teddy feels warm."
2. (encouraging) "Teddy! Yes! Give teddy a hug! Squeeeeze!"
3. (wait 2s) (playful prompt) "Teddy says brrr! What do we do? Hug!"

**Screen:** Teddy bear photo with a shivering wobble animation; when child responds, a warm orange glow wraps around teddy with a small floating heart.

#### Step 3: Multi-Round Interaction

**Round 1 — "Teddy needs a pat":** (simple, high-success — build confidence)

**AI says:** (gentle storytelling) "Oh no! Bonk! Teddy sad. What say?"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) "It's okay, teddy!" / "Pat pat!" / "Don't cry!"
2. (Unexpected) "Ow!" / "Bonk bonk!" / makes a sound
3. (No response) Child is quiet or looking at screen.

**AI follow-up:**

1. (proud whisper) "Pat pat. Teddy better. So kind."
2. (validating) "Bonk! Poor teddy. Say, 'It's okay.'"
3. (wait 2s) (soft prompt) "Teddy is sad. Can you say 'pat pat'?"

**Screen:** Teddy photo with a tiny red bump overlay and tear drop; when child responds, a bandage sticker appears and teddy smiles with small sparkles.

**Round 2 — "Teddy needs to sleep":** Child soothes teddy to sleep with "night night" or "shhhh"; AI whispers back and teddy gets a blanket animation with lullaby notes.

#### Step 4: Celebration

**AI says:** (warm, proud celebration) "Wow! You helped teddy! Best Helper!"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) Child giggles, smiles, says "Yay!" or "I'm the helper!"
2. (Unexpected) "More!" / "Again!" / "Teddy wake up!"
3. (No response) Child is quiet, smiling.

**AI follow-up:**

1. (beaming) "The best helper ever! Teddy loves you."
2. (playful) "Teddy says thanks! Help again tomorrow!"
3. (gentle, warm) "Teddy is smiling at you. You did it!"

**Screen:** Golden badge reading "Teddy's Best Helper" with a teddy icon and heart, confetti animation, and the teddy photo looking cozy below.

#### Step 5: Closing + IB Concepts

**AI says:** (warm, reflective) "Teddy gives hugs. That's Function! You cared for teddy. That's Responsibility!"

**Child responses:**

1. (Ideal) Child smiles, says "Bye teddy!" or hugs their actual teddy.
2. (Unexpected) "Teddy is my friend!" / any response.
3. (No response) Child is quiet.

**AI follow-up:**

1. (soft, closing) "Bye bye, teddy! Sweet dreams. See you next time!"
2. (warm) "Yes! Teddy is friend. Friends help."
3. (gentle close) "Night night, teddy. Night night, helper!"

**Screen:** "Function" with a hugging-teddy icon and "Responsibility" with a gentle-hands icon appear artistically; teddy photo centered below wrapped in a cozy blanket with soft golden glow.
